Summary & Team

Project: Combine Multiple Importance Sampling with ML-driven adaptive sampling to reduce noise in path tracing by focusing rays on perceptually important regions.

Team: Eduardo Cortes, Yuhe Qin, Zhehao Yang, Henry Michaelson

Problem Description

Path tracing noise persists in scenes with complex lighting. MIS balances light and BSDF sampling, while adaptive sampling targets high-variance areas. We propose using ML saliency to guide both sample allocation and MIS weights for faster convergence.

Goals & Deliverables

Planned

  • Implement BSDF and light sampling
  • Integrate MIS with power/balance heuristics
  • Develop ML-driven adaptive sampling
  • Benchmark across strategies

Stretch

  • Complex scene extensions
  • Dynamic MIS weight adjustments via ML
  • Visualize saliency & sampling maps
